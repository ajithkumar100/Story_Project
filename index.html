LOKESH CINEMATIC UNIVERSE

Lokesh Cinematic Universe (LCU) is an Indian shared universe of action thriller films created byLokesh Kanagaraj.The first feature of the universe Kaithi was released in 2019 and the second installment Vikram was released on 3 June 2022.The universe follows an elaborate conflict between law enforcement officers and vigilantes in South India who are at war against a dangerously powerful drug cartel called Vetti Vagaiyaara. It is currently the 4th highest grossing Tamil film franchise.



Lokesh kanagaraj

Lokesh Kanagaraj is an Indian film director and screenwriter who works in Tamil films. He started his career with a short film in the 2016 anthology Aviyal. He later directed his first feature film Maanagaram (2017). He also created the Lokesh Cinematic Universe franchise with his films Kaithi (2019) and Vikram (2022). His upcoming film, Leo, marks his second collaboration with Vijay after Master (2021).

Kaithi

The first installment of the LCU, it stars Karthi in the lead role, along with Narain, Arjun Das, Harish Uthaman, George Maryanand Dheena in pivotal roles. The story follows Dilli, an ex-convict, endeavors to meet his daughter for the first time after 10 years imprisonment. However, a drug raid that Inspector Bejoy orchestrates interrupts Dilli's attempts.

Vikram

The second installment in the LCU stars Kamal Haasan, Vijay Sethupathi and Fahadh Faasil with Kalidas Jayaram, Narain and Chemban Vinod Jose in supporting roles and Suriya in a cameo role. The film is a spiritual successor of Vikram (1986). The story follows a black ops squad led by Amar, who is assigned to track down a group of ruthless vigilantes. When Amar investigates the case, he realises it is not what it seems to be, and following down this path will lead to nothing, but war between everyone involved. The film introduced "Rolex" played by Suriya and reveals that the smuggling van carrying 900 tons of cocaine which was captured by Inspector Bejoy and his team belong to Rolex as he is the boss of Anbu, Adaikalam and Sandhanam, thus making him the overarching villain of the universe.

LEO

The third installment in the LCU titled Leo (also marketed as Leo: Bloody Sweet) is an upcoming Indian Tamil-language action film directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj, who co-wrote the script with Rathna Kumar and Deeraj Vaidy. It is produced by Seven Screen Studio and The Route. The film stars Vijay, leading an ensemble cast that includes Trisha, Sanjay Dutt, Arjun Sarja, Gautham Vasudev Menon, Mansoor Ali Khan and Mysskin. The film was officially announced in January 2023 under the tentative title Thalapathy 67, as it is Vijay's 67th film as a lead actor, and the title of the film was announced a few days later. Principal photography commenced the same month in Chennai along with a sporadic schedule in Kashmir. It is scheduled to conclude in mid-July. The film has music composed by Anirudh Ravichander, cinematography handled by Manoj Paramahamsa and editing by Philomin Raj.

In May 2021, the film's producer, S.R.Prabhu, confirmed the sequel was in the works.[citation needed] Kaithi 2 is intended to be a prequel for Dilli, focusing on his character. Later, in an interaction with fans through Twitter, he explained about Anbu's return in Vikram, as he was knocked out and not dead, though he had stitch marks in his jaw, due to the injuries. Following the success of Vikram, Lokesh planned to expand the scale of the sequel, with S. R. Prabhu admitting that it would be bigger than the first film.Production of the film was expected to begin in the second quarter of 2022, but was delayed as Lokesh intended to resume the project after completing his planned project with Vijay, titled Leo.

ROLEX

In Sassoon Docks, Mumbai, Anbu, and Adaikalam arrive with their men. They meet up with the gangsters affiliated with Sandhanam and hold a meeting with their boss, Rolex. Adaikalam and Anbu reveal Dilli's involvement in the Trichy drug ambush, and Sandhanam's men reveal Vikram and Amar's involvement in destroying their drug syndicate. An epilogue shows that Amar, after Gayathri's death, joins Vikram's gang to continue taking down drug syndicates after her funeral in Ernakulam. Sandhanam's men also reveal the current location of Dilli in Uttar Pradesh and Vikram's family in San Francisco. Rolex announces a massive bounty for the execution of Amar, Dilli, and Vikram's team. Unknown to Rolex and everybody else at the meeting, Vikram is also present at the meeting and learns about the bounty placed on all of them
